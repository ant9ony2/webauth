The forest stretched endlessly, a vast expanse of green crowned with sunlight filtering through the thick canopy. The air was rich with the scent of pine and damp earth, punctuated by the occasional trill of birdsong. Beneath the towering trees, a soft carpet of moss cushioned every step, while tiny streams wove silver paths through the undergrowth. Time seemed to slow here, each moment suspended in a delicate balance of stillness and life. It was a place where the world’s chaos faded, leaving only the quiet harmony of nature’s rhythm.
