Visit to Eiffel Tower

October 14, 2018
I visited the Eiffel Tower in Paris, France. Unluckily it rained that day so I had to bring an umbrella. The eiffel tower is a lot bigger than I thought it was. It took hours to walk up the step on the eiffel tower. The view at the top definitely made it worth it though.

Visit to Queen's Apartments

October 15, 2018
The next day I decided to visit the Queen's Apartments. It was very pretty & a lot larger on the inside than it looked on the outside. As I was touring the inside of the buildings, a guide informed me that the project of Haussmannization was the reason for this building's construction. My feet hurt a lot after walking around so much.

Visit to the Royal Chapel

October 16, 2018
The next day, as I was traveling to the Royal Chapel, I was called a Flâneu, which apparently means traveler. When I got to the Royal Chapel I was stunned by the beautiful architecture of the building. The inside was even better, it was so large I almost felt uncomfortable.
