Directions

New York City is located in the southeastern corner of New York State, at the meeting point of the Hudson River and the Atlantic Ocean. It is approximately 90 minutes south of Albany, the state capital, and about four hours southwest of Boston. The city is spread across five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island. Positioned just a short drive from major landmarks like Niagara Falls to the north and the nation's capital, Washington, D.C., to the south, New York City is a central hub for travel and commerce in the northeastern United States.
